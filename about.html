About YouTube Tracker

Built by creators, for creators

Our Story

We started YouTube Tracker because we needed a better way to monitor our own video performance. YouTube's native analytics are powerful, but they have delays and aren't optimized for real-time monitoring. We wanted instant notifications, automatic updates every 15 minutes, and beautiful charts that actually help us understand our growth.

Our Mission

To empower content creators with real-time insights into their YouTube performance. We believe every creator deserves access to professional-grade analytics tools, regardless of their channel size or budget.

Why We Built This

The YouTube Creator ecosystem needed a simpler, more accessible alternative to expensive analytics tools. We built YouTube Tracker to be:

Simple

No complicated features. Just the stats you need, presented clearly.

Reliable

Running 24/7 with 99.9% uptime. You can count on us.

Affordable

Free for everyone to start. Premium for power users.

Fast

Results in milliseconds. No lag, no delays.
